Carbon Zerow

One boat. Four rowers. Carbon neutral Atlantic crossing. On the 28th of January 2018 Team Carbon Zerow arrived at English Harbour in Antigua after safely rowing across the Atlantic in 45 days while remaining friends to the end! This is our story and a record of our crossing.
